Lino Application Analyst
========================

**Requirements**

- You have a good knowledge of the existing Lino applications.

- You understand why people use these applications.

- You can test and evaluate a new version of the application.

- You are able to speak with local system administrators and users
  about their problems and needs.  If there is something you don't
  understand, you formulate questions which they understand.
 
- You live near `Eupen <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eupen>`__ and
  speak German and/or French.
