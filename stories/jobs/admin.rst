.. title: Lino System Administrator
   

**Work**

Rumma & Ko also offers hosting for Lino applications to their
customers.  Your job is to take care of the maintenance and security
of those servers. You will receive root access to our servers. We will
give your phone number to our customers and you will respond to their
problems, asking other team members for help if necessary.


**Requirements**

- Previous experience with managing Internet servers using `Apache
  <https://httpd.apache.org/>`_

- Lino is written in Python. You don't need profound knowledge of the
  language, but you are going to install Python packages (using `pip
  <https://pip.pypa.io/en/stable/>`__ into virtual environment (using
  `virtualenv <https://virtualenv.pypa.io/en/stable/index.html>`__).

- If you have previously hosted `Django
  <https://www.djangoproject.com/>`_ applications, then you will feel
  at home.

- You must be able to validly sign a `Confidentiality Agreement
  <http://www.saffre-rumma.net/dl/confidentiality_agreement.pdf>`__.
